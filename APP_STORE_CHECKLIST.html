Goal: get build 19 into the hands of external beta testers. Apple reviews once per build group (~1–2 days).

Xcode Organizer

🛡️ Review Positioning

IPTV apps get extra scrutiny from Apple. Here's how Apex is positioned to pass review:

ConcernHow Apex Handles It
"Does it stream pirated content?" No. Apex is a player only — zero bundled channels or playlists. Users supply their own authorized credentials. Anti-piracy policy at ANTI_PIRACY.md.
"Is it just a white-label player?" No. Custom theme system, EPG guide, Skip Intro, For You recommendations, cross-device sync, Stremio support — substantial original features beyond basic playback.
"Why 17+ rating?" User-supplied streams may include unfiltered third-party media. Parental controls are included (child profiles, category hiding) but don't lower the store rating.
"Are IAPs for content?" No. Apex Pro unlocks features (unlimited playlists, downloads, profiles, Trakt, For You). Never content. This is allowed under AGPL.
"Does it use private APIs?" No. Standard SwiftUI, SwiftData, AVKit, StoreKit 2, CloudKit. No private frameworks.
